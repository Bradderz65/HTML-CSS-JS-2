My Story

How I came to create this resource for our community

I was a normal(ish), happy-go-lucky kinda guy until I came off my motorbike, in spectacular fashion, in July of 1999.

I broke my back in three places, T4, 5 & 6.... my neck in three places, C3, 4 & 5.... smashed my left knee.... broke my clavicle.... and a rib or two.... punctured a lung.... and died for longer than is recommended.... somewhere between 5 and 15 minutes. The end result being that I was left paralyzed from the chest down and reliant on a wheelchair for getting about.

I won't bore you with all the reasons that I had to count my blessings.... suffice to say that my philosophy on life is such that we should all play the cards that life deals us to the best of our ability. So I set about becoming a happy-go-lucky paraplegic.

How do I get my love life back on track?

The most important, and overriding question being.... After many hours of trawling the net looking for information and inspiration.... and coming to dead end after dead end. I decided to create a website that catered for people that found themselves in a similar position.

If you don't find answers to any of the questions that you have, just contact us, and we'll do our very best to supply you with the answer.

Medical Treatments & Aids

Comprehensive information about treatments and devices for erectile dysfunction

Viagra (Sildenafil Citrate)

Probably the best known tablet for erectile dysfunction. Made by Pfizer, comes in blue tablet form in strengths of 25mg, 50mg and 100mg.

Duration: Approximately 30 minutes

Important: Take no more than 1 in a 24 hour period

Note: Also available in oral jelly form. Recreational drugs can have negative effects.

Erec-Aid (Vacuum Pump)

A vacuum pump device that creates suction around the penis, drawing blood into it, then trapped with tension rings.

How it works:

  1. Vacuum cylinder with tension ring(s) is placed over the penis
  2. Pump removes air from cylinder, causing erection
  3. Tension ring is slid to base of penis; cylinder is removed

Important: Only leave bands on for 30 minutes maximum. Can be tricky at first but gets easier with practice.

Cialis

Another effective medication for erectile dysfunction, similar to Viagra but with different duration and characteristics.

Consult with your healthcare provider for detailed information about dosage and usage.

Alprostadil

An injectable treatment option for erectile dysfunction that works directly at the injection site.

Requires proper medical guidance and training for safe administration.

Penile Implant

A surgical solution for erectile dysfunction when other treatments are not effective or suitable.

Requires consultation with a urologist specializing in implant procedures.
